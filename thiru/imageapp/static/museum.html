KRISHNAGIRI

GOVERNMENT MUSEUM

Major Exhibits and GalleriesThe museum balances cultural history with interactive science models, making it highly popular for school excursions and families:Archaeology & History: Houses ancient hero stones (including the 1062 AD Bimandappalli Hero Stone depicting Sati from the Chola era), centuries-old terracotta figurines, palm-leaf manuscripts, and ancient roof tiles.Historical Weaponry: Displays 16th to 19th-century military equipment including swords, daggers, stone cannonballs, and gunpowder guns alongside photographs of local historic forts like Maharajakadai and Jegadevi.
